WELCOME TO TETRIS

STEP1

Start the game on the lowest level. Tetris game provides a Marathon Mode that lets you pick your level, which will determine how fast the tetrominos fall. The speed of the tetrominos fall will increase every time you complete one row to give you a challenge. There are 3 levels: beginer,medium and advanced

STEP2

Move the tetrominos. You can move them left and right using left and right arrow, and you can rotate them in both directions using up arrow. [up is clockwise], and You can also speed up their fall,known as "Soft Drop" used with the down arrow.

STEP3

Understand the different tetrominos. There are seven different types of tetrominos: the I, O, L, J, S, Z, and T tetriminos. You are able to choose the tetrominos you want to play with.

STEP4

Complete lines to gain points and raise the level. When a line of blocks is created, the entire line disappears, points are rewarded and the stack is pushed down. The more lines completed at once, the more points gained. Completing the maximum number of lines at once, four, is known as a 'Tetris' and can only be pulled off with the I-tetrimino.

STEP5

Check upcoming pieces and plan for them. This will become easier the more you play, don't rush yourself.
